hello!

My name is Tina and I am a front-end developer! I am a recent graudate of Juno College of Technology's Web Development Bootcamp. I love to solve problems and create aesthetic applications with clean and re-useable code.

Prior to transitioning to tech, I worked in the medical communications industry as a client and project manager. This experience has helped me develop strong communications skills and the ability to effectively problem solve in a fast-paced and high-pressure environment. I hope to use my skills to develop engaging applications tailored to each client's needs.
