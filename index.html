hello!

My name is Tina and I am a front-end developer! I am a recent graduate of Juno College of Technology's Web Development Bootcamp. I love to solve problems and create aesthetic applications with clean and re-useable code.

Prior to transitioning to tech, I graduated with an MSc in Immunology from the University of Toronto, and was working in the medical communications industry as a client and project manager. This experience has helped me develop strong communications skills and the ability to effectively problem solve in a fast-paced and high-pressure environment. I hope to use my skills to develop engaging applications tailored to each client's needs.

Outside of coding, you can find me trying new recipes and attempting to keep my plants alive. Feel free to reach out if you are ever in the mood for cool science facts, are in need of a developer, or just for fun!
